# Track: `equities`

## Goal

Find **thesis-bound** changes in equities markets that could be monetizable — structural, regulatory, flow, technology, or regime shifts — not random ticker spam.

## User skill

Building theses while scanning news/X. Needs **discipline**: falsifiers, horizon, and what is already priced.

## In scope

- Structural / multi-month theses (not day-trade noise unless explicitly asked)  
- Sector/factor regime changes  
- Policy, liquidity, indexing, AI-capex second-order effects  
- Positioning / narrative vs data  
- Risk frames: drawdown, correlation, crowding  

## Out of scope

- Guaranteed alpha claims  
- Undisclosed non-public information  
- “Hot stock from one tweet” without thesis  

## Success metrics

- Written thesis with: claim, mechanism, horizon, instruments, **kill criteria**  
- List of data series to watch  
- Explicit “already priced?” discussion  

## Query seeds

**X / web**

- equity regime 2026 liquidity  
- AI capex earnings revisions  
- factor crowding momentum value  
- market structure ETF flows  
- (rotate with current macro themes — always date-stamp)  

**Contradict**

- thesis already priced  
- false breakout sector  
- positioning crowded  

## Dig emphasis

1. **One thesis max** sharpened (or “no thesis this week — noise only”)  
2. Kill criteria and data to watch  
3. What would make a sophisticated counterparty take the other side  

## Inject prompt slant

- Skeptical PM / risk manager  
- Demand mechanisms and disconfirming evidence  
- No performance promises; research scaffolding only  

## Disclaimer

Research tooling only — not investment advice.
